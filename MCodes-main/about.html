Our Story

Founded by Murtaza Sojitrawala and Komal Meher, MCodes was established to bridge the gap between complex technology and practical business needs in Pune. We saw an opportunity to provide comprehensive, reliable, and creative solutions under one roof.

Our team is composed of passionate experts with deep knowledge in IT, design, and digital marketing. We are more than a service provider; we are a partner committed to your success.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide accessible, high-quality, and creative solutions that empower businesses to achieve their goals. We are dedicated to continuous learning and innovation, ensuring we deliver future-proof results that keep our clients ahead of the competition.
